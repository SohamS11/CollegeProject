What is JEE Main?
JEE Main is a national-level entrance exam for admission to B.E/ B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Plan courses offered by 31 NITs, 25 IIITs, and 19 GFTIs. The exam is conducted by NTA, twice every year. Session 1 of the exam is conducted around January and Session 2, around April. For admission to IITs, candidates must clear JEE Main and appear in the JEE Advanced exam.

    JEE Main 2024 Eligibility

    Candidates must know the JEE Main Eligibility Criteria specified by NTA before registering for the exam. 

    • Educational Qualification: Pass 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized Board.
    • Compulsory Subjects: Study Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in classes 11 and 12.
    • Minimum Marks: Score a minimum of 75% marks (65% for SC/ST candidates) in Class 12 board exams. 
    • Age Limit: There is no age limit criteria for JEE Main.
      • However, IITs have their age criteria that candidates must fulfill for admission.
    • Attempts: A maximum of three consecutive attempts in JEE Main.
      • JEE Main is conducted twice every year. Candidates can appear for both sessions which will be considered a single attempt. The best score will be used for admission.

      Ques. How to complete JEE Main 2024 registration?

      Ans. JEE Main 2024 registration is done online through the JEE Main NTA official website. Candidates need to fill out the JEE Main application form, upload the required documents, and pay the fee to successfully register themselves for the exam.

      Ques. Is the JEE Main syllabus different for Paper 1 and Paper 2?

      Ans. Yes, Paper 1 tests subjects Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Paper 2 has questions from Mathematics and an aptitude test. It also has a section for the Drawing test/Planning test depending on the Paper the candidate has chosen in the JEE Main application form.

      Ques. Can one make JEE Main application corrections after fee payment?

      Ans. Yes, NTA opens the JEE Main application correction window after the last date of application submission. Candidates who have made any mistake in their application can make corrections within the deadline set by NTA.

      Ques. Is there any change in the JEE Main 2024 syllabus?

      Ans. As of now the syllabus for JEE Main is the same as in past years.

      Ques. Who will conduct JEE Main 2024?

      Ans. Both sessions of JEE Main are conducted by NTA every year. However, JEE Advanced is conducted by one of the 7 zonal IITs, each year on a rotational basis.

      Ques. What is a good score for JEE Main?

      Ans. A score above 250 or higher is good enough to secure a seat in a prominent engineering institute.

      Ques. How do I download the JEE Main scorecard?

      Ans. Candidates will be able to download the JEE Main scorecard from the official website of jeemain.nta.nic.in after JEE Main result is announced.

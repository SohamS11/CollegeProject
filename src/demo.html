SunRise University, Alwar, Rajasthan was established in 2011. It is a public university, set up by the IET Group of Industries. The university has been approved by UGC and AICTE. At present, Sunrise University offers UG, PG, Diploma, Certification and PhD courses in various fields including Yoga and Naturopathy, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Engineering, Business Management, Science and Arts. Admission to the courses is based on merit secured in the qualifying exam and entrance exam.

SunRise University Admissions

SunRise University does not conduct any entrance exam for UG, PG, Diploma and certification courses. Candidates are shortlisted based on merit secured in the last qualifying exam. Whereas, admission to PhD is conducted on the basis of a university entrance test followed by PI.

How to Apply?

Students interested in applying for SunRise University can follow the admission procedure given below:

  • Visithttp://www.sunriseuniversity.in
  • Download the online application form by selecting ‘download admission form’ from the dropdown menu.
  • Students can also procure a hard copy from the college’s admission department.
  • Add your details including name, address, date of birth, parent’s information and educational qualifications.
  • Attach attested copies of all documents including ID proofs, address proof, educational qualifications and any other certificates.
  • Submit the application form to the admissions department on the campus.

Documents Required

The candidate will be required to submit self-attested copies of the following documents for admission:

  • Birth certificate
  • Proof of identity and Address (Aadhaar card, ration card, driving license, etc)
  • Proof of Education Qualification (10th and 12th marksheets and certificates)
  • Passport size photographs (12)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Provisional Certificate if the candidate does not have their 12th marksheets and certificates.

SunRise University Facilities

The university campus is 30 acres of green land with trees, plantation and well-maintained greenery. The facilities found on the campus are as follows:

  • Hostel:SunRise University provides hostel facilities for out-of-town students. Girls and boys have separate hostel facilities which can be availed throughout the year. The hostel also provides high-speed internet and other amenities.
  • Laboratories:SunRise University has state-of-the-art laboratories for R&D and pharmacy. The laboratories are equipped with high-speed internet and the latest sophisticated pieces of equipment which the students can use.
  • Computer Labs:The university provides Hi-tech computer labs with licensed software and networking peripherals. All students can use this facility during college hours. The IT blocks are also equipped with the latest teaching aids.
  • Library:The campus also has a central library with books and journals. The library has a subscription to various national and international journals and newspapers. It also contains educational CDs for students and teachers.
  • Auditorium:The University has an on-campus auditorium with a capacity of 1000 students. The auditorium is used to hold convocation ceremonies and various cultural events.
  • Gymnasium:The university gymnasium is used to train students and hold college sports events. The gymnasium also includes working equipment for boys and girls including a treadmill.
  • Arcade:The arcade contains shops where students can buy stationeries, take printouts or xerox and STD/PCO service. It also contains an attached modern cafeteria with Juice machines.
  • Medical Centre:The University has a full-time medical centre with an in-house doctor and nurse facility. The centre also has one ambulance that is always available for service.

SunRise University Faculty

The University has a highly qualified faculty on campus that is committed to providing students with an all-around education. The institute has 14 departments with each department having its heads of the department, lecturers, associate professors, assistant professors, junior lecturers and visiting faculty.

SunRise University FAQs

Ques. Does SunRise University have high-speed internet?

Ans. The university campus internet service has a speed of 10mbps. The internet is accessible in all laboratories and lecture halls.

Ques. Is there Training and Placement cell in SunRise University?

Ans. Yes, the university does have a training and placement cell that works to provide students placements. The cell holds summer training seminars and project work to teach interpersonal and industry skills.

Ques. Does SunRise University provide distance education?

Ans. SunRise University does not provide distance education for any courses. Students are expected to attend on-campus courses.

Ques. Are there any cultural events for students at SunRise University?

Ans. SunRise university holds various functions. The university organizes annual fests, orientation programs, fresher and convocation days for their students. Additionally, the university also celebrates national cultural events like Diwali.

Ques. Does SunRise University offer Shuttle service?

Ans. SunRise university provides shuttle service for students and faculty alike. The transportation facility is free of charge for all and helps to commute easily.

Ques. How is the library of SunRise University?

Ans. The library at SunRise university provides access to national and international newspapers and journals. It also has books, CDs and instruction manuals. Students can avail these services once they have obtained a library card.
